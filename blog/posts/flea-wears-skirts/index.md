As a bass player, I have always been a fan of Flea, the bassist for the Red Hot Chili Peppers. I’m not a huge fan of their music, but I still think the bass lines are absolutely killer. While learning the bass for their song, “Look Around”, I decided to watch a live video of them in concert. That was when I noticed something entirely unexpected: Flea wears skirts on stage.

That may not be news to some of you, but it was certainly a big surprise to me! I love seeing men wearing skirts in places with high visibility and considering how big of a band the Red Hot Chili Peppers is, the stage is definitely a highly visible spot.
